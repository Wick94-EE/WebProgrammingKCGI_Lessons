Overview

Mr. Wick was born on May 31, 1994, in Colombo, Sri Lanka. He started his school career in 1999 and successfully graduated from DS Senanayake College, Colombo in 2013 as a High School Graduate. He entered Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology(SLIIT) University in 2014 and successfully graduated in 2019 with a degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering. He is currently pursuing a Master's in Tourism Information Technology at Kyoto College of Graduate Studies for Informatics(KCGI) University, Kyoto, Japan.
